Describing things

Thai adjectives sit straight after the noun — no "is" needed: "bâan yài" = "house big" = "the house is big."

How to drill this

  1. Describe today's actual weather out loud each morning — real conditions stick better than a random drill.
  2. Point at five objects near you right now and say their colour using "sǐi ___," then add อ่อน/เข้ม to shade it.
  3. Take one adjective a day and attach it to three different nouns — "yài" works on a house, a dog, a problem.

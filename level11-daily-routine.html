⚠ your first real paragraph

This is the level that proves the whole suite works as one system. Take any three routine phrases, add a time marker from Level 10, and a time-of-day word from Level 2 — and you have a real, connected description of your day, built entirely from templates you already know.

How to drill this — the capstone version

  1. Narrate today, start to finish, using only Thai: six to eight sentences, morning through night, no English filler words.
  2. Reuse yesterday's narration but change the tense with jà (Level 10) — turn it into tomorrow's plan instead.
  3. Once this feels natural, go back to any earlier level and fold one of its templates into your daily narration — that's the suite working as intended.

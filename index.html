Twelve levels · templates, not grammar

Thai Templates Suite

A fill-in-the-blank path through Thai, one topic at a time — every page uses the same tone-colour key and device text-to-speech, so the mechanics stay familiar even as the subject changes.

How to use this: work roughly in order the first time through — later levels (verbs, daily routine) lean on vocabulary from earlier ones. After that, jump to whichever topic you need.

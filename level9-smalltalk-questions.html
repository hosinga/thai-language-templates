⚠ "___ reu yang" — a template inside a template

หรือยัง (rěu yang, "...yet?") tacks onto almost any verb to ask whether something has happened yet: "gin-kâao rěu yang" (eaten yet?), "mii faen rěu yang" (have a partner yet?), "dtàeng-ngaan rěu yang" (married yet?). Questions about age, marriage, and money are standard opening small talk here, not the taboo topics they'd be back home — don't read them as prying.
